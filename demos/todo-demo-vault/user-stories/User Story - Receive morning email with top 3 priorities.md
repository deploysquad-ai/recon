## Story

As an Overwhelmed Individual, I want to receive a morning email with my top 3 priorities, so that I start my day knowing exactly what matters most.

## Rationale

The digest creates a daily ritual outside the app. It reaches users before they're in reactive mode and primes their intent for the day.
