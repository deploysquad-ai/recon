## Description

Focus is a personal task management app designed for people who feel buried in their to-do lists. Users add tasks, group them into projects, and the app surfaces a single priority at a time — nudging them away from multitasking. A daily digest email delivers their top 3 priorities each morning. A future team mode lets coworkers share projects and coordinate work.

## Scope

In scope: task and project management, focus-one-thing-at-a-time nudging, daily digest email, team project sharing (v2). Out of scope: time tracking, calendar integration, native mobile apps (PWA first), billing/payments in MVP.
