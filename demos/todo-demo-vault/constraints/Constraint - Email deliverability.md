## Description

Daily digest emails must reliably reach users' inboxes. This requires proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC configuration, a reputable sending IP, low unsubscribe rates, and email content that avoids spam triggers.

## Scope

Applies to the Daily Digest module. Governs choice of email provider, sending practices, and template design.
