## Description

Nudge users to work on one thing at a time, creating a habit of deep focus rather than context-switching across many tasks.

## Success Criteria

- 60%+ of users use focus mode at least 3 days per week
- Average task context switches per session drops over time
- Users rate focus quality 4+ out of 5 in feedback
