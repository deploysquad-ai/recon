## Description

Help users feel in control of their workload by presenting a manageable, focused view of their tasks rather than an anxiety-inducing list.

## Success Criteria

- Users report feeling less overwhelmed after 1 week of use
- Average open task count per user stays below 20 active items
- Users complete at least 3 tasks per week
